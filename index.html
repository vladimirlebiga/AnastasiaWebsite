Hello beautiful Lady,

I'm Anastasia, your wedding stylist. I am from Ukraine , now living in the Netherlands. I'm 32 yars old, and for 12 years, I've dedicated myself to creating beauty for my clients, a profession I adore. I'm perfectionist and Virgo, a great combo in my opinion. So, high standards for my work and the best results are important to me. I live by my work. The looks I create become inspirations and go viral on Pinterest.
